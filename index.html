About Me

Welcome to the personal website of Kaveh Moradkhani, a passionate and dedicated Ph.D. student in Computer Science at École de technologie supérieure in Montreal. Kaveh's research focus lies at the intersection of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Image Processing, with a particular interest in medical image processing. With a strong commitment to advancing healthcare technology, Kaveh's current research revolves around developing Deep Learning Methods for Accurate Brain Morphometry from Magnetic Resonance Imaging, aiming to mitigate algorithmic bias and enhance segmentation. Through rigorous academic training, extensive research experience, and a solid foundation in programming languages such as Python, C++, and Java, Kaveh possesses the expertise to tackle complex challenges in this field. With a drive for continuous learning, strong problem-solving abilities, and a keen eye for detail, Kaveh Moradkhani is poised to make significant contributions to the exciting and ever-evolving domains of Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Image Processing, ultimately striving to improve medical diagnostics and revolutionize healthcare practices.
